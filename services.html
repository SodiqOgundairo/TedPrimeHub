Our Services

You are welcome to explore services we offer at TedPrime Hub and see how we are championing exciting innovations in Tech

STEM Her

An Initiative to close the gender gap by equipping 300 Girls and 150 Female Science Teachers on STEM framework through a multi-level partnership for sustainability.

We train Girls and the female science teachers on coding, robotics and app development.

CodeLiners

Promoting kids and teens interests in STEM education by teaching them how to code using software like Arduino, micro:bit, and also preparing them for a career in web development.

TeachRight

Training/Capacity building workshop on 21st century learning design and technology in education for teachers

EduBox

World's best educational resources packaged together for places without internet thereby improving access to up to date instructional contents.

STEM MakerSpace

An experiential learning hub for age group 6-17 on STEM

Enrol your child at a world class virtual and real world of activities on physical computing with Micro:bit and Arduino, coding to solve real world problems, building robots using robotics kits, creative design with 3D printer, STEM home experiments with STEM kits, VR/AR learning and many more..

Microsoft Imagine Academy

A complete technology education solution connecting staff, teachers, students, employers and local communities through leading-edge technology skills development.

A complete framework for students in high schools and tertiary institutions to empower them with tech and productivity skills needed to thrive in the new world.
